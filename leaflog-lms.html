⏱ 14 min 📖 Intermediate 👁 2,341 views 📅 Updated Mar 2025
About this lesson

In this lesson, we explore the science and practice of crop rotation — one of the oldest and most effective tools in sustainable agriculture. You'll learn how rotating crops across seasons replenishes soil nutrients, breaks pest and disease cycles, and leads to consistently higher yields without chemical inputs.
